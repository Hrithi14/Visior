Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password? ‚ñº
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive an email with a reset link. For security, this action is logged on blockchain. If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.
What is blockchain verification? ‚ñº
Every action (login, data access, edits) is recorded on Hyperledger Fabric or Ethereum blockchain. This creates an immutable audit trail for regulatory compliance. Each transaction has a unique hash that can be verified on the blockchain explorer.
How long do access requests take? ‚ñº
Access requests are reviewed within 3-5 business days by the data access committee. You'll be notified by email once reviewed. The review process and decision are recorded on blockchain for transparency.
What is Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)? ‚ñº
MFA adds an extra layer of security by requiring a second verification method (authenticator app, SMS code, or FIDO2 key) in addition to your password. This is mandatory for all WISER accounts to ensure secure access to sensitive clinical data.
How is patient data protected? ‚ñº
All patient data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 encryption. Access is strictly controlled through role-based permissions, and all access attempts are logged on blockchain. The platform is fully HIPAA compliant and meets 21 CFR Part 11 requirements.
